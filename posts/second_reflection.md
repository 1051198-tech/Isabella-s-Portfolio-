Game Description/features: My game has many different elements and features to it such as stores, pauses and unique character descriptions. The player can start by choosing one of the three cat characters. Each character has there own backstory and special movement.In the fight the player must complete there moves to deal damage on the opponent. To complete the move the player must press all buttons before the timer runs out.Each of the three levels has an decrease in time making it hard to win each fight. After every game the player will collect 50 dollars worth of the games currency "bones". The bones can be used in the store to upgrade skills like player damage, player heath, and time to click buttons. Some extra features is the pause button which stops the game and give the player 2 options. The player can choose to go home or choose to continue the game. There is also a how to play button on the front screen that describes the elemens of the game.
